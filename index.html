The gallaxy of the year

What is this all about?

NGC 203 is a lenticular galaxy located approximately 233 million light-years from the Solar System[2] in the constellation Pisces. It was discovered on December 19, 1873 by Ralph Copeland.[3] The galaxy is also listed as NGC 211 in the New General Catalogue.
